Financing

Our financing policy includes deals with purchases. If you purchase a year's worth supply of pet food at a time, the supply will be 25% discounted and we will offer delivery options which are discussed below.

Delivery

Durham House of Pets offers delivery services to customers living in the Durham, and Dover areas. We charge $5 per delivery and encourage 20% gratuity with every delivery. We also offer scheduled deliveries so you never have to worry about running out of your favorite DHP products!

Return Policy

Durham House of Pets has a 30 day-money back guarantee on all normally priced products. As long as you have your receipt and the products are unused we will refund the entire amount to the original form of payment.
