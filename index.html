History

Welcome to Durham House of Pets located in the quaint town of Durham, New Hampshire. We are a family owned business that was founded in January 2000 by Jackson Marshall. When we first opened we were primarily focused on dogs since the founder, Jackson was a dog lover. Since then we have expanded into selling not only dog supplies, but cat and snake supplies as well. As our business grows we hope to start selling products to accommodate more pet owners. We are hoping that within the next three years we will begin to sell bird and fish supplies, along with fish as well
